Protagonist

Driver

The nameless Driver works as a car mechanic and stuntman during the day, and at night he transports robbers from crime scenes.

He works anonymously, never helping the same people twice. He is only a driver and does not take up arms. The condition for cooperation is one: five minutes are allocated for the robbery, after which he leaves.

The Driver is attentive to details. He masterfully hides from the chase, despite the fact that his car has already been noticed, and the police are pursuing him.

The Driver is a loner with no family or friends. The only person with whom the main character communicates is Shannon, in whose auto repair shop the Driver works.
